Matcha and White Chocolate Cookies Recipe : The Best It Gets

Make this recipe now!

This delicious Matcha and White Chocolate Cookie will be a hit for your next party. Inspired by Japanese Matcha Boba, this cookie will feature a soft crumble and satisfying interior creaminess from the decadant white chocolate enrobed inside. Enjoy! (P.S. Best Served with A Cold Glass of Milk!)

Ingredients:

  • 2/3 cup sugar/canesugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted, softened butter
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons AP flour
  • 10 tablespoons green tea powder (matcha)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon bakng powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup white chocolate chips
